This meal is perfect for someone who is busy and doesn't always have time to leave the office during lunch. Only 4 ingredients and takes less than 5 minutes to prepare

Ingredients

  • 1 Flour Tortilla
  • 1 Slice Havarti Cheese
  • 2 slices roasted turkey breast (deli sliced)
  • 1 persian cucumbeer, sliced down the length.

Steps

  1. Place tortilla on plate or counter. Cut cheese in half and place along center of tortilla. Top with turkey slices and cucumber.
  2. Roll tortilla tightly and slice in half.

Nutrition Snapshot: Per servinge: 366 calories, 14g fat, 6g saturated fat, 28g protein, 29g carbs, 3g fiber, 567mg sodium.
