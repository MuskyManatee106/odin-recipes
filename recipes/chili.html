Quick and Good Chili

There is a misconception that making good chili is an all day affair. With this recipe you can accomplish what takes people all day, in 25 minutes. Preepared in 5 minutes, and serves 6 cups of chili.

Ingredients

  • 1lb pkg ground beef or turkey. 85%lean or as desired.
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1(14.5oz) container Mirepoix (secret weapon, because dont have to chop onion, celery and carrot)
  • 1(15 oz) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup Chunky Salsa or your favorite salsa
  • 1 (16-oz) can Refried Blak Beans with Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Heat oil in saucepan or pot over high heat. Add mirepoix: cook and stir for about 5 minutes or until veggies are softened.
  2. Add beef: cook and stire, breaking up the beef until it is cooked thoroughly. Stir in black beans and salsa
  3. Add referied beans and 1/2 cup water; dyitr until well combined. if chili is too thick, add water a small amount at a time until you reach a desired consistency.
  4. Simmer covered over low heat for 10 to 15 minutes.

Nutrition Snapshot

Per serving: 347 calories, 14g ft, 5g saturated fat, 24g protein, 32g carbs, 5g fiber, 4g sugar, 589mg sodium.
